Tuition: LKG - 12th STD

Comprehensive academic support is provided for students from LKG up to 12th Standard, covering all subjects for both **CBSE** and **STATE BOARD** curricula with experienced faculty.

Abacus Training

Enhance mental calculation speed and accuracy through our Abacus training program, designed to boost concentration, memory, and overall brain development in young learners.

Hindi Exam Level Preparation

Dedicated coaching for various Hindi proficiency and examination levels, focusing on reading, writing, grammar, and conversation skills required to excel in formal language exams.

Drawing & Sketching

Unleash creativity through structured Drawing classes covering foundational techniques, sketching, and coloring to help students express their imagination and develop fine motor skills.

Western Dance

Explore movement and rhythm with specialized classes in both **Western** dance forms, focusing on technique, expression, and physical discipline for all age groups.

Flute Instruction

Learn the beautiful art of playing the Flute. Our music classes cover basic fingering, breath control, and proper technique to help students master this expressive instrument.

Veena Classes

Master the Veena, one of the most ancient and revered Indian string instruments. Our instruction covers traditional finger techniques, composition, and raga fundamentals.

Carnatic Music (Vocal)

Learn the principles of South Indian classical music. Our vocal classes focus on *sruti*, *raga*, and *tala*, offering a strong foundation in this rich musical tradition.

Otomatiks Robotics

Our Otomatiks Robotics program helps students learn the basics of robot building, coding, sensors, and electronics while developing creativity, logical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Chess Classes

Develop strategic thinking and problem-solving skills through our engaging chess classes. Students will learn game strategies, tactical moves, opening principles, and endgame techniques while improving concentration and decision-making abilities.

Carrom Board Classes

Improve focus, precision, and strategic thinking through our Carrom classes. Students will learn proper striking techniques, board control, and game strategies while enjoying this classic indoor sport in a fun and competitive environment.

Spoken English Classes

Improve your communication skills and confidence with our Spoken English classes. Students will learn correct pronunciation, vocabulary, sentence formation, and everyday conversation through interactive activities and practical exercises.

About

Winstar Academy functions as a comprehensive educational centre with a tuition division and an integrated play school (My Chhota School).
